Essential Tools to Manage Your Plants

Say goodbye to scattered plant photos and random watering reminders. Plant-it simplifies plant care, providing a clean, efficient way to manage your green space.

Event Tracking & Reminders

Schedule actions like watering and fertilizing, view events in a built-in calendar, and track plant care history. Stay organized and ensure your plants get the care they need.

Extensive Plant Database

Quickly expand your plant collection by searching through a database of over 400,000 species via FloraCodex. Can’t find what you're looking for? Add custom plants tailored to your collection.

Detailed Plant Profiles

Record key details like purchase date, price, and location. Upload photos, track growth, and access species-specific care information all in one place.
